# Visual Production Style Guide

This style guide details rules for generating visual assets using Veo 3.1 and Imagen.

## 1. Aspect Ratio and Formats
- **Wide (16:9)**: Reserved for YouTube campaigns and website header videos.
- **Vertical (9:16)**: Standard for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
- **Square (1:1)**: Used for standard Instagram and LinkedIn feed posts.

## 2. Branding Guidelines & Apparel Asset Mapping
- **Product Overlay**: Brand product images or logos must be mapped onto the generated video. Ensure overlays use realistic shadows and blending parameters to look natural.
- **Color Palette**: Prompt engineering must enforce the brand's primary and secondary brand colors (configured dynamically in Firestore per campaign).
- **Virtual Personas**: Avoid generic stock photos or unpolished characters. Personas generated should represent diverse, professional, and photorealistic representations that fit the brand demographic.

## 3. Cinematic Video and Synchronized Audio (Veo 3.1)
- Video generations must output high-fidelity footage (up to 4K resolution) with smooth framerates (min 30fps).
- **Audio Overlays**: Ensure synthetic voice narration matches the duration of the video. The script generated by the copywriter must be segmented to align with visual scene transitions.
