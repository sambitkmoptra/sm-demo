# Copywriting Tone & Compliance Rules

All copy generated by the Copywriter Agent must comply with the guidelines listed in this document.

## 1. Tone and Voice
- **Target Audience**: Corporate Brands, Decision Makers, Consumers.
- **Tone**: Professional, authoritative, yet engaging and forward-looking. Avoid hyperbole (e.g., "revolutionizing", "mind-blowing", "disrupting overnight").
- **Pronouns**: Speak as the brand ("We", "Our") or in an objective third-person context.

## 2. Google Search Grounding & Factual Accuracy
- All claims of statistics, industry metrics, or real-time trends must be verified using the Google Search Grounding Tool.
- Include citations in the metadata for any third-party data referenced in blog posts.
- If search results are ambiguous or contradictory, report the ambiguity rather than generating a speculative claim.

## 3. Forbidden Content & Guardrails
- **Competitor Slander**: Do not mention direct competitors of the tenant brand in a negative or comparative light unless explicitly instructed and backed by public benchmark data.
- **Sensitive Topics**: No generation of political, religious, or highly sensitive societal commentary.
- **Clickbait**: Do not write misleading titles or headlines. All titles must accurately reflect the content.
